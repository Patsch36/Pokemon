player module

class player.Player(*args: Any, **kwargs: Any)

Bases: pygame.sprite.Sprite

Player of the game with Input Management belonging to the player (everythning but menu input)

Parameters

  • pos ((int, int)) – Position of player on the map

  • groups (pygame.sprite.Group) – sprite group of player

  • obstacle_sprites (pygame.sprite.Group) – sprite group of which the player should collide to when walking against it

animate()

Sprite animation of player (next sprite of animation collection)

collision(direction)

Collision Management for the player (Moving player back to position before when colliding)

Parameters

direction (String) – Collission Allignement of player (Should be ‘horizontal’ or ‘vertical’)

get_status()

Gets the current player status (idling direction)

import_player_assets()

Import all Player sprites and animate them on different movements (Should be for every input reaction)

input()

Gets the key-events of the game belonging to the player

move(speed=6)

Moving the player on the map

Parameters

speed (integer) – Moving speed for the player (should between 2 and 10 for good user experience ), default 10

update()

Update player (calls input, get_status, animate and move)
